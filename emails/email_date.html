How it works

We will send you a parcel with an electricity recorder, which is very easy to attach to your mains electricity meter. We also give everyone in your household an activity recorder. With a few simple clicks you can tell us about the main things you do throughout this one day. When done, you post everything back to us in a pre-paid envelope and we will send you a visualisation of your activities and electricity use. Find out more here.

Your right to withdraw

Participation is entirely voluntary. You can withdraw from the study at any time and have your records deleted, simply by emailing. Or you can unsubscribe here.

We do, of course, hope that you will help us with this important research. There is also a chance to win a year free electricity.
