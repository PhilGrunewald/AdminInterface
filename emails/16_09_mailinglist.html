Dear [name],

I wanted to give you a brief update about the Meter project. We are nearly one year in and a lot has happened.

Here are some highlights:

  • Over 400 households registered within weeks of launching the project in May
  • So far we collected over 6 million electricity readings (nearly 2000 hours worth)
  • 1250 activities have been translated from booklets to the database, with lots more in progress
  • We are looking for a Data Scientist to join us (Deadline 14 Oct). Please share with people who might enjoy this.
  • The app is in the final stages of testing. It will make activity recording and coding significantly easier.
  • A visualisation interface is under development. This will allow you to explore electricity profiles and activities.

The attached "Meter Reading" gives a summary of the recent activities and explains how the app works.

We will be hosting a second 'Expert Workshop' in late November/early December. If you are keen to attend, please let me know and tell me whether there are dates we should avoid.
