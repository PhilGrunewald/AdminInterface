Dear [name],

Thank you for being part of the Meter project. We are still developing the project and hope to be able to share your first annotated load profile with you soon. Here are some highlights of recent developments:

  • Over 400 households registered within weeks of launching the project
  • So far we collected over 6 million electricity readings (nearly 2000 hours worth)
  • 1250 activities have been translated from booklets to the database, with lots more in progress
  • We are looking for a Data Scientist to join us (Deadline 14 Oct). Please share with people who might enjoy this.
  • We are in the final stages of testing an app, which will make activity recording much easier
  • A visualisation is being developed to allow you to explore your profile and activities

You can read more about this in the attached Meter Reading.
